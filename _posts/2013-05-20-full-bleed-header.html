Typography exists to honor content.

- Robert Bringhurst

Let your typography serve your content. Everytime you set a font-size to less than 16px you're telling your audience you don't really care if they read it or not. Don't stand in the way of the content you worked so hard to generate. Let it breath. Set it big or don't set it at all.
